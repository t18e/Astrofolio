On some metalinguistic level, there's a massive difference between hobbies, interests and skills. Hobbies are undertaken for pleasure and often performed routinely (doing) whereas interests often reflect domains or fields which capture our curiosity (feeling). There's no prerequisite or barrier for entry with most hobbies & interests. Skills, however, reflect acquired abilities or developed capacities to perform specific tasks with competence.
